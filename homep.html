★BRIEF OVERVIEW★

Our section, 8-Diamond, has 30 students, including 17 males and 13 females. We are supported by one adviser and co-adviser. Our section, 8-Diamond, is part of a special program focused on journalism. We always participate in DSPC (Division Schools Press Conference ), further enriching our skills and experiences in the field. Our classroom is located within the physics laboratory, features five tables where six students are seated at each, fostering collaborative learning and engagement.
